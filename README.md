# Easy Meal Recipe

---

A web app which gives you variety of different recipes for your ingredients. 

 - Enter any one ingredient that you have in search box (like Eggs, Chicken, Onion) and click on search.
 - After you get your results, you can click on Get Recipe button to view the detailed particular Recipe.
 - After clicking on the button, you will get the detailed instructions on How to make that particular Meal/Dish.
 - You can also watch the youtube video to get to know more about the Meal preparation.
